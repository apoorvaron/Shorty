To shorten a link, follow these steps:
  1. Go to the homepage of the link shortening website.
  2. Locate the input field provided, where you can enter the original long URL.
  3. Enter the long URL into the input field.
  4. Click on the "Shorten" button or a similar button to initiate the link shortening process.
  5. After a brief moment, a shortened link will be generated.
  6. The shortened link will be displayed below, and you can simply copy it to your clipboard.
  7. The shortened link is now ready to be shared or used as needed.
